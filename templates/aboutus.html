FAQs

Is there a limit on the file size I can upload?

DataSense currently has a file size limit of 100 MB per upload. If you need to upload larger files, please contact our support team for assistance.

Can I upload multiple files at once?

Yes, DataSense supports uploading multiple files simultaneously. Simply select all the files you want to upload using the file picker dialog.

Do you offer any discounts for bulk uploads?

Yes, we offer discounts for bulk uploads based on the volume of data you upload. Please reach out to our sales team to discuss your specific requirements and pricing options.

How long are my uploaded files stored on DataSense?

Your uploaded files are stored on DataSense servers for a period of 30 days from the date of upload. After this period, the files are automatically deleted from our servers for security and privacy reasons.

Can I cancel or delete my uploaded files?

Yes, you can cancel or delete your uploaded files at any time. Simply log in to your DataSense account, navigate to the "Upload History" section, and select the files you want to cancel or delete.

What happens if there is an error during file processing?

If an error occurs during file processing, our system will automatically log the error and notify you via email. Our support team will investigate the issue and provide assistance to resolve it as quickly as possible.

Do you offer custom data processing solutions?

Yes, we offer custom data processing solutions tailored to meet the specific needs of your business. Whether you need custom data transformations, data cleansing, or integration with third-party systems, our team of experts can help you achieve your objectives. Please contact us to discuss your requirements in detail.
